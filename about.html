Hatha Yoga

Vinyasa Flow
Dynamic yoga class synchronizing breath with movement, promoting flexibility, strength, and mindfulness.
Ashtanga Yoga

Kundalini Yoga
Spiritual practice combining dynamic movements, breathwork, meditation, and chanting to awaken inner energy.
Bikram Yoga
Hot yoga practice consisting of 26 postures and two breathing exercises performed in a heated room to detoxify the body and increase flexibility.
Yin Yoga
Gentle practice focusing on deep stretching and longer-held poses to release tension in the connective tissues and promote relaxation.

Comfortable, breathable clothing that allows for a full range of motion is recommended. Yoga-specific attire such as leggings, tank tops, or athletic shorts are popular choices. Avoid clothing that is too loose or restrictive.
